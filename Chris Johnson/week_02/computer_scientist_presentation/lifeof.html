Life of Hal Finney

Hal Finney was born in Coalinga, California on May 4th 1956. He attended the California Institute of Technology, graduating with a BS in engineering in 1979.

    He was best known for:

  • Being the second developer to join the PGP Corporation after the inventor Phil Zimmermann and is often credited for the invention of PGP’s “Web of trust” system.

  • Inventor of reusable proof-of-work and using it to create the first cryptographically verifiable digital currency (bitcoin without the decentralized database).

  • First person other than Satoshi Nakamoto to join the bitcoin development team, mine bitcoin and was the recipient of the first bitcoin transaction.

  • Early and influential member of the cyberpunk community.

Hal Finney was diagnosed with ALS in 2009 and died of related complications on August 28th, 2014 at the age of just 58.
