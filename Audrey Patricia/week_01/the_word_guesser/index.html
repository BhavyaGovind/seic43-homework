The Word Guesser

Play on the console!

Instructions:

To play use commands on the console:
wordToBeGuessed("string"); --> add the word to be guessed
startGame(); --> to start the guessing game
guess("char"); --> to test your guesses



Other variations for this game include:

  • Wheel of Fortune edition
    1. For this, enter into index.js
    2. Uncomment lines 72-111 and comment out 36-60


  • Hangman edition - 6 strikes and your out!
    1. For this, enter into index.js
    2. Uncomment lines 121-167 and comment out lines 36-60
