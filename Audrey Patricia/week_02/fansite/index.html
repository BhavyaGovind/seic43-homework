Who is Larry?

  • Grew up in Los Angeles, then Bremtown Washington
  • Studied Chemistry, Music and Pre-med at Seattle Pacific University
  • Graduated with a bachelor'degree in Natrual and Artificial languages
  • At Berkley in graduate school, Larry and Gloria wanted to find an unwritten language and create a writing system for it
  • He worked for the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory for a while
  • Created the widely used patch program (a tool in unix)
  • Created the Perl programming language
  • Has a witty and often sarcastic sense of humour
